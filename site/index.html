♦ FAQ

The questions that come up most.

Short answers. Message us for the full story.

We offer comprehensive caravan and trailer services including mechanical servicing, electrical and solar installations, safety inspections, wheel bearing service, brake repairs, water system overhauls, lift kit installations, woodrot repairs, and insurance accredited caravan and trailer recoveries including cross-border recovery.

Our service packages start at R949 for a single unbraked axle, R1,499 for a single braked axle (most popular), and R2,299 for a double braked axle. All packages include safety and electrical checks, brake inspection, and bearing service. Parts are quoted separately with no hidden costs.

Yes. We are insurance accredited for caravan and trailer recoveries. We handle breakdowns, accidents and roadside recovery nationwide as well as cross-border recoveries to Botswana, Mozambique, Zimbabwe, Namibia and Eswatini. WhatsApp us for immediate assistance.

Most caravan services and repairs are completed same or next day. Larger jobs like full electrical rewiring, solar installations or structural woodrot repairs may take 2–3 days. We'll give you an accurate timeline when you book.

Absolutely. We install complete solar systems for caravans including panels, charge controllers, battery banks (lithium and AGM), inverters, and full 12V/220V wiring. We'll design a system to suit your off-grid needs and budget.

252 General Beyers Street, Pretoria North. Open Monday to Friday 08:00 – 17:00. Closed on weekends. We also offer on-site services at your location for a call-out fee.
